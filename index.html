Welcome to UAV&V

UAV&V is a cutting-edge tool developed by CISUC at the University of Coimbra. It is designed to enhance the dependability and fault-tolerance of UAV systems through innovative algorithms and comprehensive simulations. Explore our tool, team, and publications to learn more about our contributions to UAV technology.

About UAV&V

UAV&V stands for UAV Verification and Validation, offering advanced methods for anomaly detection, fault classification, and dependability analysis in UAV operations. Our goal is to make UAV systems more reliable and robust in various scenarios.

Key Features

  • Real-time fault detection and classification
  • Simulation of fault scenarios
  • Comprehensive analysis and reporting
  • Modular and extensible architecture
